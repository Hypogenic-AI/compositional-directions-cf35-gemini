# Downloaded Papers

1. [The Linear Representation Hypothesis and the Geometry of Large Language Models](park2023_linear_representation_hypothesis.pdf) (2023)
   - Authors: Kiho Park, Yo Joong Choe, Victor Veitch
   - Key: Foundational formalization of LRH using causal inner products.

2. [The Geometries of Truth Are Orthogonal Across Tasks](azizian2025_geometries_of_truth_orthogonal.pdf) (2025)
   - Authors: Waiss Azizian et al.
   - Key: Demonstrates task-specific truth directions and failure to generalize.

3. [Algorithmic Primitives and Compositional Geometry of Reasoning](lippl2025_algorithmic_primitives.pdf) (2025)
   - Authors: Samuel Lippl et al.
   - Key: Shows that structural/algorithmic directions are more compositional.

4. [Stop Probing, Start Coding: Why Linear Probes and SAEs Fail at Compositional Generalisation](stop_probing_start_coding_2026.pdf) (2026)
   - Authors: Vitoria Barin Pacela et al.
   - Key: Challenges linear accessibility under superposition and compositional shifts.

5. [PolySAE: Modeling Feature Interactions in Sparse Autoencoders via Polynomial Decoding](polysae2026_compositional_sae.pdf) (2026)
   - Authors: Panagiotis Koromilas et al.
   - Key: Highlights the need for non-linear multiplicative terms for semantic composition.

6. [The Geometry of Categorical and Hierarchical Concepts in LLMs](park2024_geometry_categorical_hierarchical.pdf) (2024)
   - Authors: Kiho Park et al.
   - Key: Extends LRH to polytopes and hierarchical orthogonality.

7. [The Geometry of Truth: Emergent Linear Structure in LLMs](marks2023_geometry_of_truth.pdf) (2023)
   - Authors: Samuel Marks, Max Tegmark
   - Key: Seminal paper on linear truth representations.

8. [Comp. Gen. Requires Linear, Orthogonal Representations in Vision](compositional_generalization_linear_orthogonal_2026.pdf) (2026)
   - Authors: Koromilas et al.
   - Key: Explores similar themes in vision models.
